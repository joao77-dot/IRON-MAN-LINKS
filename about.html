About Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is the head of Stark Industries. Using his intelligence and ingenuity, he builds a high-tech suit of armor and becomes the technologically advanced superhero, Iron Man.

Stark is a founding member of the Avengers, a team of superheroes dedicated to protecting the world from extraordinary threats. Known for his wit and intelligence, Stark is a key figure in the Marvel Universe.
